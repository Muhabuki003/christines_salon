Our Story

Little Saigon's
Beauty Home

Founded in 1989, Christine's Salon has been a cornerstone of Southwest Houston's beauty community. Nestled in the heart of Alief — one of Houston's most vibrant Vietnamese neighborhoods — we've built our reputation on skill, consistency, and genuine care for every client.

From intricate nail art to precision haircuts, our experienced team brings expertise across a full range of beauty services. Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, you'll always be treated like family.

Walk-ins always welcome. Appointments encouraged for popular times.
